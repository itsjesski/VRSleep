# VRSleep – Automate Your VRChat Experience While You Sleep

Never miss a friend invitation in VRChat again! VRSleep is a lightweight desktop app that automatically accepts invites from friends on your whitelist while you're offline, changes your status to a custom one and manages your invite messages—all without using your VR resources.

## Why Use VRSleep?

- **Auto-accept invitations** – Respond to friend requests instantly based on your whitelist
- **Smart status management** – Automatically update your VRChat status when you sleep, then restore it when you wake up
- **Lightweight & efficient** – Runs quietly in the background without consuming VR resources
- **Instant startup** – The dashboard loads in seconds, ready to use
- **Customizable messages** – Create and manage up to 12 different invitation response messages
- **Secure login** – Your credentials are encrypted and never shared

## Getting Started

### Installation

1. Download the latest version from the [Releases page](../../releases)
2. Run the installer and follow the setup wizard
3. Launch VRSleep and log in with your VRChat account (2FA supported)

### First-Time Setup

1. **Log in** – Enter your VRChat username and password to authenticate
2. **Add friends to your whitelist** – Specify which friends should automatically receive acceptance responses
3. **Customize your settings** – Set your check frequency, sleep status message, and invite responses
4. **Start the automation** – Turn on the auto-respond feature and you're done!

## How to Use

### Managing Your Whitelist

Your whitelist determines who can receive automatic invitation responses. You can add:
- **VRChat usernames** (display names)
- **VRChat user IDs**

Open the app and add one friend per line to see automatic acceptances in action.

### Setting Your Status

Configure what VRChat displays to others:
- Set a custom "Do Not Disturb" or "Sleeping" message when you go offline
- The app automatically restores your previous status when you wake up

### Customizing Invite Messages

VRChat allows multiple invitation slots, and VRSleep lets you manage up to 12 different messages for various situations:
- "Thanks for the inv!" for casual friends
- "Come hang out!" for group activities
- And more!

## FAQ

**Do I need VR equipment to use VRSleep?**  
No! It works with any VRChat account, including desktop and VR users.

**Is my VRChat password safe?**  
Yes. Passwords are encrypted using Windows secure storage and never transmitted anywhere except to VRChat's official servers for authentication.

**What if VRChat changes their API?**  
We'll update VRSleep to maintain compatibility. Check for automatic updates in the app!

**How much does this cost?**  
VRSleep is completely free!

**Can I customize who gets auto-accepted?**  
Yes! Use the whitelist feature to choose exactly which friends receive automatic responses.

## Support & Troubleshooting

- **App won't start?** Try reinstalling the latest version from the [Releases page](../../releases)
- **Login issues?** Make sure 2FA is enabled in the VRChat app and verify your credentials
- **Need help?** Check the [Issues page](../../issues) or create a new issue with your question

## Privacy & Security

Your privacy matters. VRSleep:
- Stores credentials locally on your PC using encrypted storage
- Never uploads your data to external servers
- Only communicates with VRChat's official API
- Can be completely removed by uninstalling the app
